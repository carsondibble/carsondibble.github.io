Bob Ross

“It’s hard to see things when you are too close. Take a step back and look.”

-Bob Ross

Robert Norman Ross AKA Bob Ross remains one of the most famous and influential artists of the 20th and 21st century. Bob Ross launched the television series The Joy of Painting, which premiered in 1983 on PBS, where it would run for more than a decade and attract millions of viewers. The Joy of Painting would eventually be carried by more than 275 stations. Bob Ross spawned an empire that would include videos, how-to books, art supplies, and certified Bob Ross instructors. Bob Ross Inc. grew into a $15 million business, and Ross made an estimated 30,000 paintings during his lifetime.
