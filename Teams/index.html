Bob Ross

“It’s hard to see things when you are too close. Take a step back and look.”

-Bob Ross

Robert Norman Ross AKA Bob Ross remains one of the most famous and influential artists of the 20th and 21st century. Bob Ross launched the television series The Joy of Painting, which premiered in 1983 on PBS, where it would run for more than a decade and attract millions of viewers.
