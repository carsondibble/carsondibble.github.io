Bob's Early Life

“There are no mistakes, just happy accidents.”

-Bob Ross

Robert Norman Ross was born in Daytona, Florida, on October 29, 1942 to Jack and Ollie Ross.

Notable events of Bob's early life:
  • He cared for injured animals.
  • He dropped out of school in the 9th Grade.
  • He lost his left index finger.

    • Growing up, Bob loved nature.
